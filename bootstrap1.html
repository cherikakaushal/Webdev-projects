Benefits of Millets

Millets are a type of grain that is rich in nutrients and has several health benefits. Some of the benefits of millets include:

  • High in protein and fiber
  • Rich in antioxidants and minerals
  • Gluten-free and easy to digest
  • Can help lower cholesterol and blood sugar levels
